Travel to Paris

Info about the city

Paris is the capital of France, the largest city in this country by population. It is located on the banks of the Seine River in the northern part of the country, in the Ile-de-France region. The total area of the city is 105.4 square kilometers, the population is 2.2 million people. Paris is the main economic and cultural center of France, as well as an important political center of Europe. The headquarters of UNESCO and the International Chamber of Commerce are located here.

The city of lights and romance

Who among us has not dreamed of getting to Paris? Who hasn't dreamed of walking along the Champs-Elysees or being at the very top of the Eiffel Tower? Where the whole city is visible from, as if in the palm of your hand. One of the most famous cities in the world, Paris conquers guests from the first minute, at a glance. The turbulent past of the French capital captures and carries on its waves, revealing secrets and me with something unknown.

Arriving in the city, no matter day or night, the first thing you will see is the Eiffel Tower, which, like a guardian, has been proudly towering over the city for many years. Standing on the banks of the famous Seine, the tower is a truly fascinating sight. In sunny weather, its observation decks offer a stunning and unique view of the whole city. If the weather is cloudy, then climbing to its top, you seem to get into another, "sky-high" world. At night, the tower seems to be shrouded in radiance from millions of glowing bulbs.
